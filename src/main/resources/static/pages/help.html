Help & Guidelines

System usage guide for new staff members

🚀 Getting Started
01

Login to the System

Use your assigned username and password to access the system. Contact your manager if you don't have login credentials. The system uses secure BCrypt password hashing and JWT token-based authentication.

02

Navigate Using the Sidebar

Use the sidebar menu to access different sections of the system. The currently active section is highlighted. Your user role determines which features are available to you.

📋 Making Reservations
01

Go to "Add Reservation"

Click on the "Add Reservation" menu item in the sidebar to open the booking form.

02

Enter Guest Information

Fill in the guest's name (required), contact number (required), address, and email. The contact number must be between 7-15 digits.

03

Select Dates & Room

Choose check-in and check-out dates. The system will automatically show available rooms. You can filter rooms by type (Standard, Deluxe, Suite). Click on a room card to select it.

04

Submit the Reservation

Click "Create Reservation" to confirm the booking. A unique reservation ID (format: RES-YYYYMMDD-XXXX) will be generated automatically.

🔍 Managing Reservations
01

View & Search

Go to "View Reservations" to see all bookings. Use the search bar to find reservations by ID, guest name, or contact number.

02

View Details

Click the "View" button on any reservation to see complete booking details in a popup.

03

Cancel a Reservation

Click the "Cancel" button to cancel a booking. Cancelled reservations cannot be undone. The room will become available for other guests.

💰 Billing
01

Generate a Bill

Go to "Calculate Bill" and enter the reservation ID. The system will calculate the total amount based on: Room Rate × Number of Nights + 15% Tax.

02

Print the Invoice

Click the "Print Bill" button to print a formatted invoice for the guest. The invoice includes all booking details and cost breakdown.
